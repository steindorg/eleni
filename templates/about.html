Eleni Podara (Ελένη Ποδάρα)

Eleni was born and raised in Greece. Since a very young age she had shown interest in various artistic fields. She started training as a pianist and later on in painting. She holds an MA in Architecture from the National Technical University of Athens (GR) and an MA in Stage Design from the Theatre Department of the Academy of Performing Arts in Prague (CZ). In 2009 she was a guest lecturer in the School of Fine Arts of Athens.

Eleni is a theatre artist. She translates characters, ideas and symbolisms into spatial installations and costumes. The main focus of her work is to support dramatic situations through the manipulation of materials and shapes.

Her diverse background and education has led to her involvement in projects that span the spectrum of the performing arts. Previous positions have included Stage and Costume Designer in performances, Art Director in Short films, music videos, and TV shows. In Dec 2013 and Jan 2014 she was resident artist at Samband Íslenskra Myndlistarmanna.

Eleni lived in Athens, Stockholm, Paris, Prague and she is currently based in Reykjavik.
